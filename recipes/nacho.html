Nacho Cheese Sauce Recipe

You know that irresistible nacho cheese sauce you get at bowling alleys, ball parks, and stadiums? Believe it or not, you can replicate that delicious flavor in the comfort of your own home with this nacho cheese sauce recipe!

Ingredients

  • Butter and flour: This nacho cheese starts with a roux made of equal parts butter and all-purpose flour.
  • Milk: You'll need a cup of whole milk.
  • Cheese: For this classic nacho cheese recipe, opt for sliced processed American cheese.
  • Salt: A pinch of salt enhances the savory flavor of the nacho cheese.

Steps

  1. Make the roux with butter and flour.
  2. Pour in the milk and whisk until thick.
  3. Add cheese and salt, then cook until the cheese is melted.
